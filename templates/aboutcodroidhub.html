About CodroidHub

CodroidHub Private Limited is a fast-growing EdTech and IT solutions company with a strong presence in India and Nepal, dedicated to transforming education and technology through innovation, collaboration, and industry partnerships.

Mission

To empower learners, educators, and organizations by providing accessible, industry-relevant, and technology-driven education solutions, fostering innovation and creating a skilled workforce prepared for the challenges of the digital era.

Vision

To become a global leader in EdTech and IT innovation, bridging the gap between education and industry by building a sustainable ecosystem of learning, technology, and entrepreneurship that impacts lives across borders.

The company specializes in:

  • EdTech Solutions: K-12 STEM & coding curriculum, industry-aligned skilling programs, and AI/ML upskilling initiatives.
  • IT Services: Web & mobile application development, game design, and AI/ML-driven enterprise solutions.
  • Training & Skilling Programs: Bootcamps, summer industrial training, workshops, and hybrid learning models with hands-on projects.
  • Industry Integration: LMS-driven learning journeys, global certifications, and project-based internships.

Key Takeaways

  • 15+ collaborations with leading colleges and schools for curriculum integration, research support, and skilling programs.
  • 5+ global IT industry partnerships driving innovation in AI, data analytics, and product development.
  • Expertise in Generative AI, Cloud Computing, Big Data, AR/VR, and Cybersecurity.
  • Flagship initiatives like Skill Yatra, AI for Your Work, and Coding for Schools.
  • Growing footprint across India and Nepal with educators, mentors, and industry experts.
