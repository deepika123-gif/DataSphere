What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

AI refers to machines or systems that perform tasks requiring human-like intelligence — understanding, reasoning, learning, and decision-making.

What is Machine Learning (ML)?

ML is a subfield of AI where systems learn patterns from data and improve automatically — without being explicitly programmed.

Key Differences

  • AI — Broad field of building intelligent systems.
  • ML — Data-driven algorithms used inside AI.

Applications

  • Healthcare — diagnosis, imaging, drug discovery.
  • Finance — fraud detection, trading, scoring.
  • E-commerce — recommendations, forecasting.
  • Manufacturing — robotics, quality control.
  • NLP — chatbots, text analysis.
